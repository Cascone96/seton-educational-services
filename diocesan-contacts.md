# U.S. Catholic Archdioceses & Dioceses — Superintendent Contact Directory

## HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

This document lists **every Latin Church archdiocese and diocese in the United States** (178 total: 33 archdioceses + 145 dioceses) along with the **Superintendent of Catholic Schools** contact information where available.

> **Note:** Superintendent positions turn over regularly. Where a name is listed, it was verified as of early 2026. Blank entries can be found by visiting the diocese website or calling their main number. The NCEA Diocesan Catholic School Leaders Directory is the best single source for the most current information.
