# Seton Educational Services, LLC — Service Offerings

## About Dr. Vince Cascone, Ed.D.

With over 26 years of Catholic school leadership experience — including 19 years as a principal, 7 years as a diocesan superintendent, and recognition as both the NAESP and NCEA National Distinguished Principal of the Year — Dr. Vince Cascone brings unmatched expertise to Catholic school leaders at every stage of their journey.

---

## SERVICE AREA 1: Individual Principal Coaching & Consulting

### A. New Principal Onboarding Program
**For:** First- and second-year Catholic school principals
- Structured 12-month coaching engagement
- Monthly one-on-one coaching sessions (virtual or in-person)
- 90-day entry plan development
- Stakeholder communication strategies
- First-year survival guide and resource toolkit
- On-call support between sessions

### B. Experienced Principal Leadership Coaching
**For:** Principals in years 3+ seeking growth
- Bi-monthly or monthly coaching sessions
- Strategic planning facilitation
- Leadership style assessment and development
- School culture and climate improvement
- Data-driven decision making
- Accreditation preparation support

### C. Principal in Crisis / Turnaround Support
**For:** Principals or schools facing urgent challenges
- Rapid-response on-site consultation
- Conflict resolution and stakeholder management
- Enrollment decline intervention strategies
- Financial sustainability planning
- School climate recovery
- Communication and crisis management

### D. Catholic Identity & Mission Integration
**For:** Principals seeking to deepen faith formation
- Catholic identity assessment and action planning
- Mission integration across curriculum and culture
- Faculty faith formation program design
- Building partnership with pastors and parish life
- Sacramental and liturgical life of the school

## SERVICE AREA 2: Diocesan / Archdiocesan School Office Programs

### E. Principal Mentoring Program Design & Facilitation
**For:** Diocesan Catholic School Offices
- Custom mentoring program design (1-year or multi-year)
- Mentor principal recruitment, training, and matching
- Monthly cohort sessions for new principals
- Program evaluation metrics and reporting
- Ongoing mentor coach support
- Annual program review and refinement

### F. Principal Professional Development Series
**For:** Diocesan Catholic School Offices
- Customized workshop series (half-day or full-day)
- Topics include:
  - Instructional leadership
  - School finance and budgeting
  - Enrollment management and marketing
  - Building effective school boards
  - Managing difficult conversations
  - Teacher observation and evaluation
  - Catholic school culture and identity
  - Strategic planning
  - Data-informed decision making
  - Pastor-principal relationships

### G. Leadership Pipeline Development
**For:** Diocesan Catholic School Offices
- Aspiring principal identification and cultivation
- Assistant principal development programming
- Teacher-leader pathway programs
- Succession planning for retiring principals
- Leadership competency framework development

### H. Superintendent / School Office Advisory Services
**For:** Diocesan Superintendents and School Office Staff
- New superintendent mentoring
- School office organizational review
- Policy development and review
- Principal evaluation system design
- Diocesan strategic planning facilitation
- School viability and sustainability studies
