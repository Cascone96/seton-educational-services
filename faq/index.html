Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Catholic school leadership consulting with Seton Educational Services.

What does a Catholic school leadership consultant do?+

A Catholic school leadership consultant provides coaching, mentoring, and professional development for Catholic school principals and diocesan school offices. Services include new principal coaching, leadership development, school turnaround support, strategic planning, and keynote speaking — all rooted in the mission of Catholic education.

Who is Dr. Vince Cascone?+

Dr. Vince Cascone, Ed.D., is the founder of Seton Educational Services. He brings 31 years of experience in Catholic education, including 19 years as a principal and 7 years as a superintendent. His school earned a U.S. Department of Education National Blue Ribbon award, and he has been recognized as both the NAESP National Distinguished Principal and the NCEA Distinguished Principal of the Year. He also teaches a Master's-level School Leadership course at Benedictine College.

Can Catholic schools use Title II funding for leadership consulting?+

Yes. Catholic schools may be eligible to use Title II, Part A (ESSA) equitable services funding to cover the cost of consulting and professional development services at no cost to the school. Contact your local public school district to learn about available Title II funds for private school principals. Read our full guide on Title II funding.

What types of schools does Seton Educational Services work with?+

Seton Educational Services works with individual Catholic school principals, diocesan and archdiocesan school offices, and Catholic education organizations nationwide. Whether you lead a small parish school or oversee dozens of schools across a diocese, our services are tailored to your specific needs and situation.

How are consulting sessions delivered?+

Coaching sessions are primarily conducted via video conference, making it easy to connect from anywhere in the country. On-site visits are also available for school assessments, strategic planning sessions, and speaking engagements. Travel costs for on-site visits are billed separately.

What does a typical coaching engagement look like?+

Most coaching packages include two virtual sessions per month, plus on-call support between sessions. For new principals, we develop a 90-day entry plan and provide a first-year leadership toolkit. For experienced leaders, we focus on strategic planning, school culture, and measurable goal-setting. Every engagement is customized to your needs.

How much does consulting cost?+

Every engagement is customized based on scope and needs, so pricing varies. We offer packages for individual principals as well as larger diocesan contracts. Many schools can access Title II funding to cover costs at no expense to the school. Contact us for a free consultation and personalized quote.
