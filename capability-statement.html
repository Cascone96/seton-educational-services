Company Overview

Seton Educational Services, LLC provides expert consulting, coaching, and professional development for Catholic school principals and diocesan school offices nationwide. Led by Dr. Vince Cascone, Ed.D. — a nationally recognized Catholic school leader — we empower principals to lead with mission, confidence, and excellence.
