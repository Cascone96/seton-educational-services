**Best for:** Smaller dioceses seeking foundational principal support

- Two professional development workshops per year (half-day each)
- Quarterly virtual check-ins with superintendent/school office
- Resource toolkit for mentors and mentees
- Annual program evaluation report

**Best for:** Mid-size dioceses wanting robust, year-round principal development
